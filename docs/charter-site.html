What it does

Everything the project needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Charter keeps every engagement, contact, and decision attached to the project — not scattered across tabs.

Lifecycle Tracking

Projects move through a fixed pipeline with stage-specific checkpoints. Always know exactly where each engagement stands and what's needed to advance it.

Contact Management

Track internal team members and external stakeholders. Every contact includes role, title, email, preferred contact method, and notes — linked directly to engagement logs.

Engagement Logs

Timestamped notes on every call, meeting, and email. Attach the contacts who were in the room. The full history stays with the project, always.

Tasks

Lightweight per-project task lists. Create them manually or let a template pre-populate them the moment a project is created. No ceremony required.

Markdown Notes

Free-form notes per project, rendered with syntax highlighting. Keep open questions, decisions, and context in one place — attached to the project, not to a doc somewhere else.

Export & Import

Export projects to JSON or CSV for backup or sharing. Import JSON bundles or CSV files to restore or migrate data. Your data stays yours.

Menu bar

Log it before you forget it.

Click the menu bar icon to instantly log an engagement, drop a task, or add a note — without opening the main window.

Charter defaults to your most recently active project. One tap to switch.
